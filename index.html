About Me

Hello, my name is David. I currently reside in beautiful Portland, Oregon. I make my best effort to stay active in the community through volunteer work. It is my goal to make meaningful connections with people as we navigate this world together. These times are filled with uncertainty, but I believe that if we draw on one another for strength, we can perservere. My hope is that a career as a programmer will allow me to meet interesting people from all walks of life, and create interesting projects, we can be proud of.

Background

Originally from Santa Fe, New Mexico, I relocated to the northwest in 2010. For many years, I have worked in the service industry as a bar manager. Doing so has allowed me to improve my social skills and helped me adopt valuable management techniques, which will aid me in a new career. In 2017 I completed my associates degree in CIS at Portland Community College. I am now actively furthering my education in the Epicodus full-time web development track.
